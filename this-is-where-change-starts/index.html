This is Where Change Starts

Take Action

Here are some things you can do.

  • Calculate your Carbon Footprint: https://www.carbonfootprint.com/calculator.aspx
  • Unplug unused electronics
  • Reduce, Reuse, Recycle
  • Travel less, and Travel Smarter: Walk, don’t drive
  • Save your leftovers for tomorrow
  • Donate unused or unneeded clothing

Educate Others

  • Send them this website
  • Share on Instagram and Twitter with #ClimateAwareness
  • Email your friends and parents information on Climax Change
  • Write a letter to your elders
  • Hold debates with your family on what actions you can take
  • Read articles and studies regarding Climate Change

Be Honest

Be honest with yourself and others. Aiming big is great, but only if it’s possible

Be Committed

If someone doesn’t listen, try again and again

Be Brave

Don’t be afraid to try new things or take risks
